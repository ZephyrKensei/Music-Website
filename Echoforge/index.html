BIO

SKOL

Introducing "Echoforge" - a Scandinavian metalcore band from the frozen tundra of Norway, formed in 2022.

The band's sound is a fusion of crushing riffs, powerful drumming, heavy basslines, and a mix of clean and harsh vocals that blend to create a sonic landscape that is both brutal and beautiful.

Lead vocalist and songwriter - Bjorn Vikstrom - brings raw energy to the mic, drawing inspiration from the harsh winters and mystical landscapes of his homeland. Guitarists - Lars Fjell and Ethan Vex - trade off between crushing rhythms and soaring leads. While bassist - Max Wellspring - provides the thunderous foundation. Drummer - Kristian Bergstrom's - pummeling beats drive the band's intense live performances.

With their debut album "Glacier's Edge", Echoforge is poised to conquer the global metalcore scene with their unique Scandinavian sound.
